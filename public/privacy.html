Privacy Policy for TA BILL

Last updated: January 2026

This application ("TA BILL") is built for official use to manage Tour Allowances and Inspection Diaries.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect or process the following types of data to provide the app's functionality:

  • Images and Files: The app allows you to upload photos or documents (e.g., for inspection reports or profile avatars). These files are processed securely and are used solely for the purpose of generating your official reports.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device data (like operating system version) to ensure the app runs correctly.

2. How We Use Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Functionality: To create and export your travel allowance bills and diaries.
  • Storage: To save your reports for your own records.

3. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer.
